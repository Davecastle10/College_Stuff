## Hex numbers
1245 in denary is  
1000 100 10 1  
  1   2   4 5  = 1x1000 + 2x100 + 4x10 + 5x1
               = 1000 + 200 + 40 + 5
               = 1245

so A2F in hex is 
256 16 1  
A   2  F = Ax256 + 2x16 + 1xF  
         = 2560  + 32   + 15  
         = 2607
